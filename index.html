ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TopRacer Range

Toptracer Range revolutionizes golf practice by employing advanced ball tracking technology, offering real-time data on key metrics like ball speed and distance. Golfers can engage in virtual games and challenges for an entertaining practice session. With a global presence at driving ranges worldwide, Toptracer enhances the overall golfing experience by providing immediate feedback.

Adventure Golf

Adventure golf, also known as miniature golf, provides a whimsical and entertaining twist to traditional golf. Featuring creatively themed courses with obstacles like ramps and tunnels, it offers a family-friendly and social experience suitable for all ages. The emphasis is on fun and enjoyment, making adventure golf a popular recreational activity for individuals and groups alike.

Golf Lessons

Golf lessons focus on refining your swing technique, improving accuracy, and enhancing overall game performance. Whether you're a beginner or looking to fine-tune your skills. Instructors provide personalized guidance, addressing individual strengths and areas for improvement. Lessons cover fundamental aspects such as grip, stance, and posture, aiming to enhance consistency and control.
